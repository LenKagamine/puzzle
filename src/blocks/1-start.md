# The 15 Puzzle

The 15 Puzzle is a sliding puzzle consisting of 15 square tiles numbered from 1 to 15 placed on a 4Ã—4 grid, with one tile missing.

The goal is to slide the tiles into the blank to bring the board to its solved state, shown on the right.
